Who We Are

Founded in 1977 - Conveying Industries is the leading manufacturer in Conveyor and Palletizing solutions for bagged, bunduled, and baled products. With systems still in the field after 30 years, we continue to build our reputation with rugged, high quality conveyors and palletizers that are second to none.
